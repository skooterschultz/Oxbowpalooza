Bring a cup, bring an appetite

Food & Drinks

  • We will provide beer, wine, and NA beverages.
  • Bring a water bottle and a drink cup to reduce waste.
  • There will be a taco food truck on site.
  • Feel free to bring your own food and favorite libations.

Room to spread out

Camping

  • 20 open acres on the property.
  • Limited RV spots with no hookups.
  • Electric for a few, first reserved.

Over by Oxbow Park

Getting There

  • Carpooling encouraged.
  • Uber and Lyft are available, but getting a ride back into town from the property can be harder.
  • If you are ridesharing home, consider scheduling your pickup in advance.
  • Use the map button when you are ready to roll.
